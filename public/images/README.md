# Image Upload Instructions

## New Arrivals Section

Upload your images to this folder with the following names:

1. **anarkali-arrival.jpg** - Anarkali suit image (recommended size: 400x560px)
2. **palazzo-arrival.jpg** - Palazzo suit image (recommended size: 400x560px)
3. **straight-arrival.jpg** - Straight cut suit image (recommended size: 400x560px)

## Image Guidelines

- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Aspect Ratio**: 5:7 (portrait orientation)
- **Recommended Size**: 400x560px or higher resolution
- **File Size**: Keep under 500KB for optimal loading
- **Quality**: High quality, well-lit product photos

## How to Add Images

1. Place your images in the `public/images/` folder
2. Name them exactly as specified above
3. The images will automatically appear in the New Arrivals banner
4. Each image is clickable and will navigate to its respective category

## Category Mapping

- **anarkali-arrival.jpg** → Links to Anarkali Suits category
- **palazzo-arrival.jpg** → Links to Palazzo Suits category
- **straight-arrival.jpg** → Links to Straight Cut Suits category
